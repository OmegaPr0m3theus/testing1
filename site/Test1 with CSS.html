Child Selectors

Article here is inside of a section, therefor the article is a child of the section. In the CSS style sheet, there is a rule where every child of a section that is an article, turns to yellow with black background and centered.

Here the article is inside of the section, so the opposite of the last paragraph, and so it will just remain white because it does not meet the article > section rule that was established earlier.

Descendant Selector

In this case there is a class="darkblue" inside a div inside a div inside a div inside an article. So, according to the descendant selector that was created (article .darkblue), everything that has the class darkblue AND is inside an article, at any level, will turn to darkblue and be centered.
